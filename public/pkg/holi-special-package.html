Holi Special Tour - Mathura & Vrindavan

Holiday: Holi Special

Duration: 2 Days / 1 Night

Start From: Mathura Railway Station

Destination: Mathura & Vrindavan

Overview of the Holi Special Tour Package

Experience the joyous festival of colors with our Holi Special Tour Package. We'll take you to the heart of Holi celebrations in Mathura and Vrindavan, where you'll witness the vibrant traditions and immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of Braj Bhoomi. Get ready for an unforgettable experience filled with colors, music, and festive cheer!

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

Day 1 - Holika Dahan (Holi Fire): Dwarkadhish Temple Dola and Mathura Vishram Ghat

On arrival at Mathura Railway Station, you'll be transferred to your hotel for check-in. As evening approaches, prepare to witness the symbolic Holika Dahan ceremony. A pyre is set ablaze, representing the triumph of good over evil, as the story of Holika and Prahlad unfolds. Join the locals in taking a sacred round of the fire, absorbing the festive atmosphere. We'll also visit the Dwarkadhish Temple to witness the Dola, a unique swing festival, and then proceed to Vishram Ghat to experience the evening aarti along the Yamuna River. This day is a beautiful blend of religious rituals and the vibrant spirit of Holi.

Day 2 - Dhulandi Holi: Dwarkadheesh Tesu Flowers / Abir Gulal Holi and Colorful Water Holi in Brij

Get ready for the main event – Dhulandi Holi! Today, the entire Braj region erupts in a riot of colors. We'll start at the Dwarkadhish Temple, where you can participate in the traditional Tesu flower and Abir Gulal Holi, a unique and eco-friendly way to celebrate. Then, immerse yourself in the lively street celebrations, where locals and visitors alike throw colored powders and water at each other, creating a joyous and unforgettable experience. The air is filled with chants of "Happy Holi" and traditional Holi songs, and groups of revelers, known as Holi Tolis, roam the streets in a playful mood. Experience the world-famous Braj Holi, a celebration of pure joy and camaraderie.
