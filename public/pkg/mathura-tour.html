Mathura & Vrindavan Tour

Holiday: Spiritual Tour

Duration: 2 Nights & 3 Days

Start From: Delhi/Agra (Pickup From Any location)

Destination: Vrindavan

Overview of the Vrindavan Tour

This 2-night, 3-day Vrindavan tour offers a spiritual escape to one of the most revered cities in India. Pickup will be from Delhi, and the tour will guide you through the vibrant temples, ghats, and holy sites of Vrindavan, offering a perfect blend of culture, spirituality, and relaxation. Whether you are a devotee of Lord Krishna or a traveler looking for peace and solace, Vrindavan is a place that promises deep spiritual experiences and unforgettable memories.

Day 1: Pickup from Delhi Or Agra and Arrival in Vrindavan

Morning: Start your spiritual journey with a comfortable pickup from your hotel or designated spot in Delhi. The journey to Vrindavan will take approximately 3-4 hours by road. Enjoy the scenic drive through the countryside of Uttar Pradesh.

On the way: You can stop for a break at a local restaurant or rest stop.

Arrival in Vrindavan: Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel. After freshening up, you can begin your visit to some of Vrindavan's famous temples and spiritual sites.

Places to Visit in Vrindavan:

  • Banke Bihari Temple: One of the most famous temples in Vrindavan, dedicated to Lord Krishna.
  • ISKCON Vrindavan: The International Society for Krishna Consciousness temple, a peaceful place to meditate and pray.
  • Prem Mandir: A stunning temple known for its beautiful light displays in the evening.

Evening: Attend the evening aarti at one of the temples or simply relax and immerse yourself in the divine atmosphere of Vrindavan.

Day 2: Full Day Exploration of Vrindavan

Morning: Start your day with a visit to some of the most important spiritual sites in Vrindavan, including the mystical Nidhivan, a place associated with Lord Krishna’s divine pastimes.

Places to Visit in Vrindavan:

  • Radha Raman Temple: A beautiful temple dedicated to Lord Krishna and Radha.
  • Rangji Temple: Known for its South Indian-style architecture, this temple is dedicated to Lord Ranganatha.
  • Vishram Ghat: A holy ghat where devotees believe Lord Krishna took rest after lifting the Govardhan Hill.

Afternoon: You may also explore local markets or visit other smaller temples that hold significance in Lord Krishna’s life.

Evening: End your day with a peaceful boat ride at Kesi Ghat or participate in the evening aarti at Banke Bihari Temple or Prem Mandir.

Day 3: Return to Delhi

Morning: After breakfast, you can enjoy some last-minute sightseeing or shopping for souvenirs in the local market.

Return to Delhi: Depart Vrindavan by afternoon and head back to Delhi. The return journey will take approximately 3-4 hours, depending on traffic.

Evening: Arrive in Delhi by evening and drop off at your designated location, marking the end of your spiritual journey to Vrindavan.

Why Choose This Vrindavan Tour?

  • Pickup from Delhi: Convenient pickup from Delhi and a smooth, hassle-free journey to Vrindavan.
  • Spiritual Experience: Visit revered temples, ghats, and places associated with Lord Krishna.
  • Perfect for Devotees and Peace Seekers: Ideal for people seeking spiritual solace and a deeper connection with divine energy.
  • Guided Tour: Expert guides will help you explore the spiritual significance of each temple and site.

Conclusion

This 3-day Vrindavan tour offers a blend of divine spirituality and a peaceful atmosphere. Whether you are a devotee of Lord Krishna or just seeking a break from the hectic pace of daily life, Vrindavan offers a retreat for the soul. From visiting iconic temples to experiencing the tranquility of the ghats, this journey will be both enriching and peaceful. Book your tour today and experience the magic of Vrindavan!
