Delhi-Agra-Jaipur Tour

Holiday: Delhi-Agra-Jaipur Tour

Duration: 3 Nights & 4 Days

Start From: Delhi (Pickup from any location)

Destinations: Delhi, Agra, Jaipur

Overview of the Delhi-Agra-Jaipur Tour

This 3-night, 4-day Delhi-Agra-Jaipur Tour offers a comprehensive experience of India’s royal culture and history. Starting from the bustling city of Delhi, you will explore the magnificent Taj Mahal in Agra, and the regal forts and palaces of Jaipur. This tour perfectly blends India's rich history, culture, and architectural marvels, making it an unforgettable journey through North India.

Day 1: Pickup from Delhi and Arrival in Agra

Morning: Begin your Delhi-Agra-Jaipur tour with a comfortable pickup from your hotel or designated location in Delhi. The drive to Agra takes approximately 3-4 hours by road. Enjoy the scenic landscape as you head toward Agra.

On the way: Stop for refreshments at a local restaurant or rest stop.

Arrival in Agra: Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel. After freshening up, head out to explore Agra’s world-famous landmarks.

Places to Visit in Agra:

  • Taj Mahal: Visit the iconic Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.
  • Agra Fort: Explore this massive fort, a fine example of Mughal architecture.
  • Mehtab Bagh: Enjoy a stunning view of the Taj Mahal from across the Yamuna River.

Evening: Enjoy a traditional Mughlai dinner at a local restaurant or your hotel. Overnight stay in Agra.

Day 2: Agra to Jaipur

Morning: Begin your day with a sunrise visit to the Taj Mahal for a mesmerizing view. Return to the hotel for breakfast and check out.

On the way: En route to Jaipur, visit Fatehpur Sikri, the abandoned Mughal city, and explore its grand palaces and mosques.

Arrival in Jaipur: Check-in at your hotel and relax. Spend the evening exploring Jaipur's vibrant local markets, famous for handicrafts, jewelry, and textiles.

Evening: Optional: Attend a cultural show or enjoy a traditional Rajasthani dinner. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

Day 3: Exploring Jaipur

Morning: Start your day with a visit to the Amber Fort, located on the outskirts of Jaipur. Enjoy an elephant or jeep ride to the fort and marvel at its stunning architecture and panoramic views.

Midday: Explore the City Palace, an exquisite blend of Mughal and Rajasthani architecture, and visit the adjacent Jantar Mantar, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Afternoon: Visit the iconic Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds) and admire its intricate facade. If time permits, explore the vibrant bazaars of Jaipur for souvenirs.

Evening: Return to your hotel for dinner and an overnight stay in Jaipur.

Day 4: Return to Delhi

Morning: After breakfast, check out from the hotel and begin your journey back to Delhi.

On the way: Optionally, stop at local attractions or rest stops for refreshments.

Afternoon: Arrive in Delhi and drop off at your preferred location, concluding your memorable Delhi-Agra-Jaipur tour.

Why Choose This Delhi-Agra-Jaipur Tour?

  • Pickup from Delhi: Hassle-free pickup and drop-off service from any location in Delhi.
  • Iconic Landmarks: Visit the Taj Mahal, Amber Fort, City Palace, and more.
  • Perfect for History and Culture Enthusiasts: Explore India’s rich Mughal and Rajput heritage, from Delhi's historic monuments to Jaipur's majestic palaces.
  • Guided Tour: Knowledgeable guides to enhance your experience at every site, ensuring a deeper understanding of India’s culture and history.

Conclusion

This 4-day Delhi-Agra-Jaipur Tour offers an unforgettable experience of India's royal history, vibrant culture, and stunning landmarks. From the iconic Taj Mahal to Jaipur’s royal palaces, this journey will leave you with cherished memories. Book your tour today and immerse yourself in the beauty and heritage of Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur!
