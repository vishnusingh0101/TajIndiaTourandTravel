Delhi to Mathura Pilgrimage Tour (2 Days)

Holiday: Delhi to Mathura Pilgrimage Tour

Duration: 1 Night & 2 Days

Start From: Delhi (Pickup from any location)

Destinations: Mathura, Vrindavan

Overview of the Mathura Pilgrimage Tour

Embark on a serene 2-day pilgrimage from Delhi to Mathura, the birthplace of Lord Krishna. Explore sacred temples, vibrant culture, and spiritual landmarks in Mathura and Vrindavan. This short yet fulfilling journey is perfect for those seeking spiritual rejuvenation and peace.

Day 1: Arrival in Mathura

Morning: Begin your spiritual journey with a comfortable pickup from Delhi. Travel to Mathura, the birthplace of Lord Krishna, a journey of approximately 3-4 hours by road.

On the way: Stop for refreshments at a local rest stop or restaurant.

Arrival in Mathura: Check-in at your hotel and relax. Begin your pilgrimage with visits to iconic temples.

Places to Visit in Mathura:

  • Shri Krishna Janmabhoomi: The sacred birthplace of Lord Krishna, a must-visit site.
  • Dwarkadhish Temple: Known for its stunning architecture and spiritual atmosphere.
  • Vishram Ghat: A peaceful spot on the banks of the Yamuna River.

Evening: Witness the evening aarti at Vishram Ghat or enjoy a traditional meal at a local restaurant.

Day 2: Explore Vrindavan and Return to Delhi

Morning: After breakfast, head to Vrindavan, a short drive from Mathura, to visit more sacred temples and landmarks associated with Lord Krishna's life.

Places to Visit in Vrindavan:

  • Banke Bihari Temple: One of the most famous temples dedicated to Lord Krishna.
  • ISKCON Temple: A serene and modern temple known for its beautiful ambiance and spiritual energy.
  • Prem Mandir: A grand temple showcasing intricate architecture and light displays.

Afternoon: After exploring Vrindavan, enjoy a local vegetarian lunch. Begin your journey back to Delhi by road, a drive of approximately 3-4 hours.

Evening: Arrive in Delhi and be dropped off at your designated location, marking the end of your spiritual pilgrimage.

Why Choose This Mathura Pilgrimage Tour?

  • Convenient Pickup: Hassle-free pickup and drop-off service from any location in Delhi.
  • Spiritual Sites: Visit the birthplace of Lord Krishna and iconic temples in Mathura and Vrindavan.
  • Short Duration: Perfect for a weekend getaway or spiritual rejuvenation.
  • Guided Experience: Knowledgeable guides to enhance your visits to sacred sites.

Conclusion

This 2-day pilgrimage tour to Mathura and Vrindavan offers a peaceful and spiritual retreat for devotees and travelers alike. Visit the sacred temples, experience the divine atmosphere, and create lasting memories. Book your tour today and embark on a journey to the holy land of Lord Krishna!
