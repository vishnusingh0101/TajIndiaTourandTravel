Taj Mahal Sunset Tour from Delhi

Holiday: Romantic & Cultural Tour

Duration: 1 Day

Start From: Delhi (Pickup from any location)

Destination: Agra

Overview of the Taj Mahal Sunset Tour

Witness the enchanting beauty of the Taj Mahal at sunset on this exclusive one-day tour. Known for its ethereal charm during dusk, the Taj Mahal radiates golden hues that create a magical experience. This tour also includes visits to Agra Fort and the serene Mehtab Bagh, ensuring a memorable evening in Agra.

Day Schedule: Delhi to Agra and Back

Morning: Begin your journey with a comfortable pickup from Delhi, Noida, or Gurugram. Travel to Agra in an air-conditioned vehicle, enjoying light refreshments during the drive.

Midday: Explore the historic Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its grand architecture and rich history. Have a delightful lunch at a local Agra restaurant featuring Mughlai specialties.

Afternoon: Head to the iconic Taj Mahal and witness its changing hues as the sun begins to set. Experience the serene beauty of the monument while capturing stunning photos during the golden hour.

Evening: Conclude your visit with a trip to Mehtab Bagh, a tranquil garden offering panoramic views of the Taj Mahal during sunset. Begin your return journey to Delhi, reflecting on the unforgettable sights and experiences of the day.

Why Choose This Taj Mahal Sunset Tour?

  • Unique Experience: Witness the Taj Mahal in its most mesmerizing state during sunset.
  • Comprehensive Itinerary: Includes visits to Agra Fort and Mehtab Bagh for a well-rounded tour.
  • Expert Guidance: Learn about the history and significance of the sites with knowledgeable guides.
  • Comfortable Travel: Travel in a private, air-conditioned vehicle with professional drivers.

Additional Information

  • Pickup is available from hotels, airports, or residences in Delhi NCR.
  • Sunset timing may vary based on the season; the itinerary will be adjusted accordingly.
  • We recommend wearing comfortable shoes and carrying a camera to capture the beautiful moments.
  • Tour availability is subject to local weather and government regulations.

Conclusion

The Taj Mahal Sunset Tour offers a unique opportunity to witness one of the world's most iconic monuments at its most magical time. With seamless travel, expert guidance, and unforgettable views, this tour promises an enriching and romantic experience in Agra.
