Same-Day Jaipur Tour from Delhi

Holiday: Historical & Cultural Tour

Duration: 1 Day

Start From: Delhi (Pickup from any location)

Destination: Jaipur

Overview of the Same-Day Jaipur Tour

Discover the charm of Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan, on a seamless same-day tour. Known as the "Pink City," Jaipur is renowned for its stunning palaces, majestic forts, and vibrant culture. From the iconic Amber Fort to the intricate Hawa Mahal, immerse yourself in a day of royal splendor and architectural beauty, all with hassle-free pickup and drop-off from Delhi.

Day Schedule: Delhi to Jaipur and Back

Early Morning: Start your journey with an early pickup from Delhi, Noida, or Gurugram. Travel in a comfortable, air-conditioned vehicle to Jaipur, approximately a 5-hour drive via the Delhi-Jaipur Expressway. Enjoy light refreshments or snacks en route.

Morning: Upon arrival in Jaipur, begin your exploration with a visit to the magnificent Amber Fort. Marvel at its grand architecture, beautiful courtyards, and stunning views of the surrounding Aravalli Hills. Experience the rich history of the fort with the help of an expert guide.

Midday: Relish a delectable Rajasthani lunch at a renowned local restaurant, featuring authentic dishes like dal baati churma, gatte ki sabzi, and more.

Afternoon: Continue your tour with a visit to the iconic Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds), known for its unique facade. Explore the City Palace, an exquisite blend of Rajasthani and Mughal architecture, and visit the Jantar Mantar, a UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring ancient astronomical instruments. If time permits, explore the vibrant markets of Jaipur for souvenirs and handicrafts.

Evening: Begin your return journey to Delhi. Relax during the drive, reflecting on the vibrant culture and royal heritage of Jaipur. You will be dropped off at your desired location in Delhi by late evening.

Why Choose This Same-Day Jaipur Tour?

  • Hassle-Free Travel: Pickup and drop-off from Delhi for a convenient experience.
  • Iconic Landmarks: Visit Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, and other historical sites in a single day.
  • Expert Guidance: Knowledgeable guides to enhance your understanding of the sites.
  • Comfort and Safety: Travel in a private, air-conditioned vehicle with an experienced driver.

Additional Information

  • Pickup is available from hotels, airports, or residences in Delhi NCR.
  • We recommend wearing comfortable walking shoes and carrying sunscreen or hats during summer visits.
  • Tour availability is subject to local weather and government regulations.
  • Special dietary requirements? Let us know in advance.
