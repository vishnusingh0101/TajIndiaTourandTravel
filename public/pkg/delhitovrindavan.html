Delhi, Agra & Vrindavan Tour

Holiday: Delhi, Agra & Vrindavan Tour

Duration: 3 Nights & 4 Days

Start From: Delhi (Pickup from any location)

Destinations: Delhi, Agra, Vrindavan

Overview of the Delhi, Agra & Vrindavan Tour

This 3-night, 4-day tour takes you to some of North India's most famous destinations. Begin your journey in Delhi, where you will visit historical landmarks like the Red Fort and India Gate. Then head to Agra to experience the beauty of the Taj Mahal and Agra Fort. Finally, immerse yourself in the spiritual charm of Vrindavan, known for its temples and peaceful atmosphere. A perfect tour for history lovers, culture seekers, and those wanting to experience India’s rich heritage.

Day 1: Pickup from Delhi and Explore Delhi

Morning: Start your journey with a pickup from your hotel or any location in Delhi. Begin exploring the major landmarks of the capital city.

Places to Visit in Delhi:

  • Red Fort: Explore this UNESCO World Heritage site, a symbol of Mughal power and architecture.
  • India Gate: Visit this war memorial, one of Delhi’s most iconic landmarks.
  • Qutub Minar: Admire the impressive 73-meter tall Qutub Minar, showcasing Indo-Islamic Afghan architecture.

Evening: Relax at your hotel or enjoy a casual stroll through Connaught Place or other popular markets.

Day 2: Travel to Agra and Explore

Morning: After breakfast, leave Delhi and head towards Agra. The drive will take approximately 3-4 hours.

Places to Visit in Agra:

  • Taj Mahal: Visit the world-famous Taj Mahal, a symbol of love and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.
  • Agra Fort: Explore the magnificent Agra Fort, showcasing rich Mughal architecture.
  • Mehtab Bagh: Enjoy a peaceful view of the Taj Mahal from across the Yamuna River.

Evening: Take a leisurely walk in Agra’s markets or enjoy a traditional Mughlai dinner.

Day 3: Travel to Vrindavan and Explore

Morning: After breakfast, travel to Vrindavan, a town famous for its temples and spiritual significance. The drive will take about 3-4 hours.

Places to Visit in Vrindavan:

  • Banke Bihari Temple: Visit this famous temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, known for its beautiful architecture and divine atmosphere.
  • ISKCON Vrindavan: Explore this well-known temple complex, part of the global ISKCON organization.
  • Prem Mandir: Visit this peaceful and picturesque temple, offering a serene atmosphere and stunning light shows in the evening.

Evening: Enjoy the tranquility of Vrindavan, perhaps visit the ghats of Yamuna River or relax in the temple complexes.

Day 4: Return to Delhi

Morning: After breakfast, you can enjoy some last-minute sightseeing or shopping in Vrindavan.

Return to Delhi: Depart Vrindavan and head back to Delhi. The journey will take approximately 3-4 hours.

Evening: Arrive in Delhi by evening and be dropped off at your designated location, concluding your tour.

Why Choose This Delhi, Agra & Vrindavan Tour?

  • Explore Diverse Destinations: Discover the vibrant capital of Delhi, the majestic Taj Mahal in Agra, and the spiritual atmosphere of Vrindavan.
  • Perfect Blend of Culture and History: Visit iconic landmarks and temples while learning about India's rich history and spirituality.
  • Comfortable Travel: Enjoy hassle-free transportation between cities with dedicated pickup and drop-off services.
  • Guided Experience: Knowledgeable guides to enhance your visit and provide insights into the significance of each site.

Conclusion

This 3-day tour of Delhi, Agra, and Vrindavan offers a perfect combination of history, culture, and spirituality. Visit the iconic Taj Mahal, explore Delhi's landmarks, and experience the peaceful aura of Vrindavan’s temples. Book your tour today and embark on a journey that blends the best of India’s heritage and spirituality!
