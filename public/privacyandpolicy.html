Privacy Policy

Welcome to Taj India Tour and Travel. Your privacy matters to us, and we are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect it. This policy aligns with applicable laws and regulations in India, including the Information Technology Act, 2000, and the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide when contacting us or booking a tour package.
  • Website Usage Data: Information such as IP addresses, browser details, navigation patterns, and device information to improve website performance and user experience.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Data that helps us personalize your experience, enhance our website functionality, and gather analytics.
  • Financial Information: Payment details such as credit/debit card information and billing addresses when making transactions, securely handled as per industry standards.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the services you request, such as booking tours or responding to inquiries.
  • Enhance our website, products, and services to serve you better.
  • Send you updates, promotional offers, or travel-related information, but only if you have opted in to receive such communications.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations in India, such as maintaining transaction records for audits or legal purposes.

Data Protection Measures

  • Using advanced encryption and secure server technologies to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.
  • Regularly updating our security protocols and conducting audits to identify and mitigate risks.
  • Restricting access to your data only to authorized personnel who need it to perform their duties.
  • Complying with industry standards and Indian regulations for data protection.

Third-Party Sharing

  • When it is necessary to provide the services you request (e.g., sharing details with a tour operator or payment gateway provider).
  • When required by Indian law, such as in response to court orders, government inquiries, or legal proceedings.
  • With trusted third-party service providers who assist us in managing our operations, subject to strict confidentiality agreements.

Your Rights

  • Access: Request access to the information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request corrections to your personal data if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to any legal obligations or requirements under Indian law.
  • Consent Withdrawal: Withdraw your consent for processing your personal data at any time, where consent is the basis for data collection.

If you wish to exercise these rights or have any questions about our privacy practices, contact us at tajindiatourandtravel@gmail.com.

Retention of Data

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, compliance requirements, or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page with the revised date. It is your responsibility to review the policy periodically.
