Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Welcome to the Taj India Tour and Travel FAQ section. Here, we’ve answered some of the most common questions our customers have. If you don’t find the answer you're looking for, feel free to contact us for further assistance.

1. How can I book a tour with Taj India Tour and Travel?

Booking a tour is simple. You can either book directly on our website by selecting a tour or package, entering your details, and making a secure payment. Alternatively, you can contact us via email or phone, and our team will assist you in making a booking.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including major credit and debit cards, net banking, UPI, and payment wallets. You can choose the option that is most convenient for you at the time of booking.

3. Can I customize my tour package?

Yes, we offer customized tour packages based on your preferences. You can choose destinations, dates, and specific services you need. Please contact us directly, and our team will help design a personalized itinerary for you.

4. How do I cancel my booking?

If you wish to cancel your booking, you can do so by contacting us via email at tajindiatourandtravel@gmail.com. Please refer to our Terms and Conditions for our cancellation policy.

5. Do I need to buy travel insurance?

While travel insurance is not included in your booking, we highly recommend purchasing it to cover unforeseen situations such as trip cancellations, medical emergencies, or loss of baggage. You can find a number of reputable insurance providers online or we can help guide you in purchasing one.

6. What happens if there’s a change in my booking or the tour package?

If there is any change to your tour package, we will inform you as soon as possible. In the case of cancellations or major changes, we will offer you the option to either select an alternative package or receive a refund, depending on the circumstances. Please refer to our Terms and Conditions for more details.

7. Is my payment secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways to ensure that your transactions are safe. Your personal and payment details are protected and handled with the utmost care. We do not store sensitive payment data on our servers.

8. Can I make changes to my booking after it's confirmed?

Changes to bookings can be made subject to availability and may incur additional charges depending on the nature of the changes. Please contact our support team as soon as possible if you need to make adjustments to your booking.

9. How do I contact customer support?

If you need help with your booking, have questions about a tour, or require any assistance, feel free to contact our customer support team via email at tajindiatourandtravel@gmail.com or call us at +91 7827000754.

10. Are the tours suitable for children or elderly travelers?

Our tours can be tailored to suit different age groups, including families with children and elderly travelers. If you have specific requirements, such as accessibility needs or family-friendly activities, please inform us at the time of booking so we can make appropriate arrangements.

11. What should I pack for my tour?

What you pack depends on the destination and type of tour you choose. We recommend packing comfortable clothing, sunscreen, travel documents, and any personal items you may need (medications, toiletries, etc.). We provide a detailed packing list for most of our tours, which you can request when booking.

12. What if my flight is delayed or canceled?

In the event of flight delays or cancellations, you should immediately inform our customer support team. We can assist in adjusting your travel plans, rebooking accommodations, or making alternative arrangements. However, please note that we are not responsible for third-party services such as airlines.
