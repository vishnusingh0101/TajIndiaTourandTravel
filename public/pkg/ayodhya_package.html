Ayodhya Tour

Holiday: Spiritual & Pilgrimage Tour

Duration: 1 Nights & 2 Days

Start From: Delhi (Pickup from any location)

Destination: Ayodhya

Overview of the Ayodhya Tour

This 2-night, 3-day Ayodhya tour offers an immersive spiritual experience in one of India’s most sacred cities. With pickup from Delhi, this tour takes you to the birthplace of Lord Ram and other iconic temples, including the majestic Ram Mandir. Experience the religious aura, explore historical landmarks, and delve into the deep cultural heritage of Ayodhya. Ideal for pilgrims and spiritual seekers.

Day 1: Pickup from Delhi and Arrival in Ayodhya

Morning: Start your journey with a comfortable pickup from your hotel or designated spot in Delhi. The drive to Ayodhya takes approximately 7-8 hours by road, crossing through scenic landscapes and towns.

On the way: Take a break at a local restaurant or rest stop to refresh.

Arrival in Ayodhya: Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel. After freshening up, begin your spiritual exploration of Ayodhya's significant landmarks.

Places to Visit in Ayodhya:

  • Ram Janmabhoomi: The revered birthplace of Lord Ram, a pilgrimage site for millions.
  • Ram Mandir: The newly constructed temple dedicated to Lord Ram, showcasing stunning architecture.
  • Kanak Bhavan: A temple dedicated to Lord Ram and Sita, steeped in cultural and spiritual significance.

Evening: Optionally, take part in the evening aarti by the Saryu River or enjoy a peaceful visit to the ghats.

Day 2: Full Day Exploration of Ayodhya

Morning: Begin your day by visiting the Chhoti Devkali Mandir, one of the most sacred temples dedicated to Lord Kali. Later, head to Saryu River for a peaceful boat ride.

Places to Visit:

  • Saryu River: A serene boat ride on the holy river, offering a calm spiritual experience.
  • Treta Ke Thakur: A historical site marking the location of Lord Ram's yajna (ritual offerings).
  • Hanuman Garhi Temple: A popular temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman, a key deity in the Ramayana.

Afternoon: Continue your visit to local temples and religious sites. You can also explore the nearby markets for spiritual souvenirs.

Evening: Optionally, enjoy a cultural performance or a visit to the Guptar Ghat, where Lord Ram is believed to have taken his final departure.

Day 3: Return to Delhi

Morning: After breakfast, enjoy some last-minute sightseeing or shopping for religious souvenirs in Ayodhya’s local markets.

Return to Delhi: Depart Ayodhya in the afternoon and head back to Delhi. The return journey will take approximately 7-8 hours.

Evening: Arrive in Delhi by evening and be dropped off at your designated location, marking the end of your spiritual pilgrimage.

Why Choose This Ayodhya Tour?

  • Pickup from Delhi: Hassle-free pickup and drop-off service from any location in Delhi.
  • Spiritual Immersion: Explore the holiest places in Ayodhya and witness religious rituals.
  • Ideal for Pilgrims: A meaningful journey for those seeking spiritual growth and connection.
  • Guided Tour: Knowledgeable guides to provide insights into the historical and spiritual significance of each site.

Conclusion

This 3-day Ayodhya tour offers an enriching experience for pilgrims and spiritual seekers. From visiting the birthplace of Lord Ram to experiencing the serene Saryu River, this tour provides an unforgettable journey into the heart of India’s spiritual heritage. Book your tour today and embrace the divinity of Ayodhya!
