Jaipur Tour

Holiday: Cultural & Heritage Tour

Duration: 2 Nights & 3 Days

Start From: Delhi (Pickup from any location)

Destination: Jaipur

Overview of the Jaipur Tour

This 2-night, 3-day Jaipur tour takes you to the heart of Rajasthan’s royal culture and history. With pickup from Delhi, this tour offers an immersive experience into Jaipur’s grand forts, palaces, vibrant bazaars, and exquisite Rajasthani cuisine. Perfect for history buffs and cultural enthusiasts, this journey will leave you captivated by Jaipur's rich heritage.

Day 1: Pickup from Delhi and Arrival in Jaipur

Morning: Start your journey with a comfortable pickup from your hotel or designated spot in Delhi. The drive to Jaipur takes approximately 5-6 hours by road. Enjoy the changing landscape as you enter Rajasthan.

On the way: Stop at a local restaurant or rest stop for refreshments.

Arrival in Jaipur: Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel. After freshening up, begin your exploration of Jaipur’s rich cultural landmarks.

Places to Visit in Jaipur:

  • City Palace: A stunning blend of Mughal and Rajasthani architecture, the City Palace is a must-visit.
  • Jantar Mantar: A UNESCO World Heritage site, this astronomical observatory showcases Jaipur’s scientific heritage.
  • Hawa Mahal: Known as the Palace of Winds, this iconic structure is perfect for photography enthusiasts.

Evening: Explore the bustling markets of Jaipur, such as Johari Bazaar or Bapu Bazaar, or enjoy a traditional Rajasthani dinner.

Day 2: Full Day Exploration of Jaipur

Morning: Start your day with a visit to the majestic Amber Fort, located on a hilltop and known for its grandeur and stunning views.

Places to Visit:

  • Amber Fort: Explore the grand courtyards, palaces, and beautiful Sheesh Mahal (Hall of Mirrors).
  • Jaigarh Fort: Famous for housing the world's largest cannon on wheels, the Jaivana.
  • Jal Mahal: A beautiful palace situated in the middle of the Man Sagar Lake.

Afternoon: Indulge in a traditional Rajasthani lunch and continue exploring Jaipur's cultural gems.

Evening: Enjoy a cultural performance or light and sound show at Amber Fort, or relax at your hotel.

Day 3: Return to Delhi

Morning: After breakfast, enjoy some last-minute sightseeing or shopping for souvenirs in Jaipur’s local markets.

Return to Delhi: Depart Jaipur by afternoon and head back to Delhi. The return journey will take approximately 5-6 hours.

Evening: Arrive in Delhi by evening and drop off at your designated location, marking the end of your cultural adventure in Jaipur.

Why Choose This Jaipur Tour?

  • Pickup from Delhi: Hassle-free pickup and drop-off service from any location in Delhi.
  • Cultural Immersion: Explore Jaipur’s iconic forts, palaces, and cultural sites.
  • Perfect for History Enthusiasts: A deep dive into Jaipur’s royal heritage and traditions.
  • Guided Tour: Knowledgeable guides to enhance your experience at every site.

Conclusion

This 3-day Jaipur tour is a gateway to the royal and vibrant culture of Rajasthan. From exploring grand forts to shopping in colorful bazaars, this tour promises unforgettable memories. Book your tour today to experience the charm of the Pink City!
